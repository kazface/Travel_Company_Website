Highlights of Switzerland

Duration: 5 days 5 nights Tour Type: natural beauty of the majestic Alps, the placid lakes, quaint villages, cosmopolitan cities and towering castles Tour Activity: mountains tour Tour Region: Engelberg & Zurich

Day 1 : Engelberg Welcoming day

Arrive in Zurich and catch the train to Engelberg (using Swiss Pass). Engelberg is a Swiss town resort and municipality located in the Obwalden canton. At a height of about 1000 meters, Engelberg is located 25 kilometers south of Lake Lucerne in a broad alpine valley. For families, newbies, and those in the know, the beautiful hamlet with the historic monastery provides a wide range of holiday activities. In Central Switzerland for the night

Day 2: Mountain Trip

Prepare yourself for a trip to Mt. Titlis. Take the train from Lucerne to Engelberg. Your roundtrip ticket includes a cable car ride from Engelberg to Mount Titlis. The glacial paradise with the world-famous ROTAIR - at 3020 m / 10,000 ft. - offers a 360-degree panoramic view of snow-covered valleys, glaciers, the Black Forest area, lakes, and mountains. On top of Mount Titlis, enjoy the snow and ice. Try the Ice Flyer chairlift (weather permitting). The view of the glacier crevasses is really breathtaking! Afternoon at leisure. In the afternoon, we travel to Lucerne. Lucerne is one of the world's most beautiful cities, with a wealth of sites and activities.

Day 3 : Rhine Falls

Today, we went out on a Swiss Pass trip to the Rhine Falls. The Rhine Falls are Europe's most powerful cascade and one of Switzerland's most famous tourist attractions. The tremendous force of the Rhine Falls astounds visitors, especially during the summer, when the water pours at a staggering 600,000 litres per second. The falls continue to flow at a rate of 250,000 litres per second during the winter.

Day 4 : Welcome to Zurich

We check out of our accommodation today and transfer to Zurich using a sector ticket. Zurich is the economic and educational hub of Switzerland. The city, which is located in the center of Europe, has all of the contemporary conveniences of a global metropolis without losing its natural beauty. Zurich has long been regarded as one of the best cities in the world in terms of quality of life. The city, which is situated alongside the glistening Lake Zurich and the Limmat River, provides a diverse range of sights and activities.

Day 5: Casual day in the city

Another day in the city, the opportunity to walk around the city and enjoy the beautiful views. After the continental dinner, departure and transfer to Zurich airport by shuttle.
