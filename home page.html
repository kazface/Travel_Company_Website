Langkawi is a peaceful archipelago of 140 islands, few of which are inhabited. The largest of them is the island of the same name, which has the status of a UNESCO Geopark, as its protected areas are densely populated with wild animals. The best way to experience this ecological wonder is from the Langkawi Sky Bridge, which stretches over the rainforest.
