Miracle cities

Malacca & KL

Duration: 5 days 5 nights Tour Type: 2 cities of Malaysia Tour Activity: Discover main sights of cities Tour Region: KL and Malacca

Day 1 KL: Welcoming day

Greetings from Malaysia! When you arrive at Kuala Lumpur International Airport, you will be greeted and taken to your hotel for check-in. Take the remainder of the day to gather your bearings and explore your surroundings. In Kuala Lumpur, spend the night.

Day 2 KL: City Center

You begin your tour of Malaysia's capital after breakfast at the hotel. Your tour of Kuala Lumpur will take you to all of the city's highlights, including the gold-domed King's Palace, the National Monument, and the National Museum, all of which were erected to commemorate Malaysia's independence struggle. Before visiting the peaceful National Mosque, Independence Square or Anglican Church, and the famous KL Tower (with entry to viewing deck) that graces every Malaysian postcard, stock up on gifts in China Town. In Kuala Lumpur, spend the night.

Day 3 KL: Genting Highlands& Batu Caves

After a delicious breakfast, depart from the Genting Highlands hilltop resort. As you ride the cable car up to the resort, take in the breathtaking sights. Genting is one of Malaysia's most popular tourist destinations, with something for everyone. Visit Snow World, a winter paradise in hot and humid climates, as well as the indoor and outdoor amusement parks, which are ideal for thrill seekers of all ages. You might even try your luck at Casino de Genting, the country's largest casino. Visit Batu Caves on the way, which is home to one of the largest Hindu shrines devoted to Lord Murugan outside of India. In Kuala Lumpur, spend the night.

Day 4 Malacca:UNESCO treasure

After breakfast, we go to Malacca, our next destination. Malacca (sometimes written Melaka) is the capital of the coastal state of Malacca, in southern Malaysia, and lies around 150 kilometers from the capital city of Kuala Lumpur. Chinatown's main avenue, Jonker Street, is noted for antique shops and its night market. Later in the afternoon, we go to Melaka Bird Park, which boasts Malaysia's largest aviary and will be the only bird park in the country to host such a diverse variety of Malaysian bird species.

Day 5 Malacca : Having fun

After breakfast, we'll splash around at 'Malaysia's Largest Water Theme Park,' as you begin your thrilling water adventure! Grab your suite and get ready for the coolest sort of summertime fun; laugh and enjoy the joy with your friends and family, and get away from it all. Then we go to Safari Wonderland, where we may go into the realm of chanting animals, view a tiger's fearsome teeth, and pet gentle bunnies all in one spot.
